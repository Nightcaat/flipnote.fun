Q: How is this different from the Flipnote Archive?
A: flipnote.studio will have far, FAR fewer Flipnotes (less than 1,000, at least at launch), but the information about them will be more accurate and complete, include PPM download links, and have MP4 versions available (which lets you do things like watch Flipnotes with your friends in VR). This project is not affiliated with the Flipnote Archive or Sudomemo team and should not be considered competition. The Flipnote Archive is still incredibly important; I highly recommend supporting it!
